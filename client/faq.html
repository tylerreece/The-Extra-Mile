Frequently Asked Questions

  1. Why am I asked to pay a transfer fee?

    The Extra Mile is currently a 501c3 pending nonprofit. While we do not have the IRS exemption to offer tax deductions on miles transfers, we will happily cover the cost! Please email your receipt to reimbursements@sendmiles.org, and we will send you a reimbursement within 3 business days.
