Our Mission

The Extra Mile Foundation is a nonprofit organization that allocates donated airline frequent flyer miles and monetary contributions towards free flights to aid veteran supporting organization.

The Problem

While working with several veteran supporting nonprofits, we realized that many companies are willing to donate towards events like tandem skydiving jumps or campground rentals; however, organizing a cost effective method of transportation to facilitate these events proved to be a great hurdle. Especially when involving physically disabled veterans, cheap alternatives such as buses were not a viable option.

The Solution

Rather than spending expiring on undesirable products, The Extra Mile Foundation allows flyers to donate to veteran empowerment campaigns of their choice.

Our Commitment

The Extra Mile pledges to offer a streamlined process by working directly with other veteran supporting nonprofits to support their logistic efforts to minimize the bureaucratic red tape veterans work through to get the help they need.
